Reshaping the Future

Yabatech Hostel is a place where students can find comfort and a sense of belonging while pursuing their academic journey. We understand the importance of a supportive and inclusive environment for students to thrive academically and personally.

Our Story

From our historic foundations in 1947 to today's modern expansions, we've spent decades shaping the ultimate hostel living experience for our students. What started as a small residence has grown into a vibrant community that houses hundreds of students from all walks of life.

Our Mission

Providing a conducive and supportive home base so students can focus entirely on their education, without worrying about where they are coming from. We believe every student deserves a safe, comfortable place to call home.

Reliable 24/7 power supply through advanced solar and electrical solutions, keeping the hostel powered always. We invest in sustainable energy to ensure uninterrupted comfort for our residents.
